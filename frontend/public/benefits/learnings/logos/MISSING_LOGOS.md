## Logo Guidelines

### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: SVG (preferred) or PNG with transparent background
- **Height**: 40-48px uniform height
- **Colors**: Original company colors (will be styled in code)
- **No shadows or effects** on the logo itself
- **File naming**: Exact company name as listed above

### Design Best Practices
- Use official brand logos from company press kits
- Ensure logos are crisp and clear
- Avoid low-resolution images
- Keep original aspect ratio
- Remove any backgrounds or padding

### Where to Find Logos
1. Company official website (usually in "Press" or "Brand" section)
2. [Brandfetch](https://brandfetch.com/)
3. [Clearbit Logo API](https://clearbit.com/logo)
4. Company LinkedIn pages
5. Official brand guidelines
